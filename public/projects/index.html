One Byte: Pizza Review Analysis Link to heading

Welcome to One Byte, a data analysis project that analyzes the data from Dave Portnoy’s One Bite Pizza series. The One Bite Pizza reviews started in 2014 and have really gained popularity since 2016, with a new review being uploaded almost daily since then. As a fan of pizza, I have always been searching for the perfect slice. That’s why I have analyzed over 1300 pizza locations reviewed by Dave Portnoy in his One Bite Pizza series to bring you the ultimate guide to finding the best pizza.

In case you’re not familiar with the One Bite Pizza reviews, they are a series of video reviews created by Dave Portnoy where he visits pizza places and scores the pizza on a decimal scale from 1 to 10. Dave has reviewed thousands of pizza spots and tried all types of pizza, making his reviews a valuable resource for anyone seeking the best pizza. The One Bite Pizza videos have had over 350,000,000 views on YouTube, making them a popular source for pizza enthusiasts everywhere. I gathered the data for this analysis by scraping the One Bite Pizza website and YouTube videos to find analytics.

In this post, I will use this data to uncover some interesting insights about the world of pizza, including the distribution of scores, the relationship between different score types, and the geographic distribution of reviewed restaurants. So grab a slice (or a whole pie) and join me as I explore the world of pizza with One Byte.
